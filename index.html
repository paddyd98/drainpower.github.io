About

O

DrainPower represents the next step in sustainable heating technology, born from the team exercise within the 2023 New Venture Creation Project (NVCP). The NVCP, which is a component of the EIT Renewable Energy Masters Programme and receives support from ESADE and EIT InnoEnergy, focuses on the creation of the Drain-Water-Heat-Pump to redefine efficiency in our homes and workplaces.

Our Technology

02

The Drain-Water-Heat-Pump (DWHP) leverages a heat exchanger to capture and reuse energy from drain water. This recovered heat elevates the temperature of a refrigerant, which is then used for building heating, offering a highly efficient alternative to traditional heat pumps, especially in colder climates.
